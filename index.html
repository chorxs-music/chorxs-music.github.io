Daniel - Main Violin






Daniel was born in Shanghai, China and is a second semester student. My principal instrument is violin. Majoring in EPD. He is a big fan of electronic music, hihop and rnb. Not a great violinist but really down with the DJ sets and Live Sets performances. Favorite artist is Holly Herndon.

Kevin - Main Keyboardist






Kevin was born in Vancouver, Canada and is The Black Hair's primary keyboardist. He attends Berklee with his two other bros and formed "The Black Hairs" on April 13th, 2023. His favorite artists prior to coming to Berklee were The Carpenters, Michael Jackson, and Coldplay.
